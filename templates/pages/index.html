Our Zanzibar

Zanzibar is an island about 23 miles off the East African Coast. Its very name evokes romantic associations - smell of spices, images of narrow streets, Arab chests, carved doors, coral reefs. It is a tourist’s paradise, with tranquil, warm tropical waters, many miles of beautiful sandy beaches on the Indian Ocean.

On these shores passed explorers like Burton, Speke, Livingstone, and Vasco da Gama in 1502 en-route to India. Zanzibar has a rich and ancient past, having been the centre for travellers sailing from distant lands of the Middle East by dhows.

Zanzibar offers a multitude of luxurious accommodation options around the island ranging from boutique private retreats to medium sized resorts. Reef Tour & Travel is here to help you plan your trip from travel logistics to accommodation and everything in between, our destination experts will craft a vacation you’ll never forget while exploring the natural wonders you’ve always wanted to see.
